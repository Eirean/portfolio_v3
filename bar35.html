Bar35 Website

Local Business Site

Thought Process

The aim of this project was to re-imagine the Bar35 home site. The original was designed using wix and whilst functional, was not memorable nor exciting. By updating the look and feel of the site, it is hoped that it will engage the user's interest and drive curiosity towards events and reservarions. By re-desiging the drinks page, we hope that a more interactive experience will encourage diversity whilst ordering. The food page is still pending images, but since Luna's Hideaway uses a different aesthetic than the bar proper, we wished to reflect that in in both type and design.
